4. SIGNATURE ATOMIC HOT DOGS (GRILL)
Atomic Inputs
  • All-Beef Franks
  • Premium Brioche Buns
  • Garlic Butter Glaze
  • Diced Onions / Relish
Commissary Prep
The Score: Cut a shallow cross-hatch (X-pattern) across the top of each frank. This allows heat to penetrate and "blossom" the meat on the grill.
The Bun: Pre-split buns and brush the interior lightly with Garlic Butter. Place in bins.
Machine: Industrial Grill
Step 1: Sear franks until the scoring opens and edges are charred.
Step 2: Flash-toast the buttered buns on the cooler side of the grill for 30s. Assemble immediately.
5. BUTTERED FOIL-LOCK CORN (BASE)
Atomic Inputs
  • Fresh Corn (Shucked)
  • Softened Salted Butter
  • Atomic Seasoning Dust
  • Heavy Duty Foil
Manual Prep Logic
The Coating: Butter every single inch of the cob. Do not miss the tips.
The Lock: Dust with seasoning. Wrap in foil and execute a "Double-Fold" seam to trap all steam inside.
Machine: Industrial Grill
Method: Place foiled ears on high heat. Rotate 1/4 turn every 4 minutes. The corn should "steam-fry" inside its own butter.
6. ELOTE STREET-STYLE FINISH
Atomic Inputs
  • Grilled Foil-Lock Corn
  • Chipotle Crema
  • Cotija Cheese Bin
  • Tajin & Cilantro
The Assembly Step
The Paint: Carefully unwrap the hot corn. Use a silicone brush to "paint" a heavy layer of Chipotle Crema over the entire surface.
The Encrusting
The Roll: Roll the creamed ear in the Cotija Cheese bin until fully encrusted. Finish with a heavy dusting of Tajin. Serve with a fresh Lime Wedge.
