Risk Management & Safety Compliance

Hygiene Architecture & Cross-Contamination Control
Gold Utensils: Dedicated to the "Sweet Suite" (Brownies, Churros, Sweet Dips). Zero contact with savory profiles.
Silver Utensils: Dedicated to "Savory Staples" (Pretzels, Popcorn, Salt Dips). Maintains flavor purity across inventory.
Red Utensils: Exclusive Path for raw/high-heat proteins (Wings, Beef Dogs). No contact with dry-good surfaces.
Glove Protocol: Task-specific Nitrile barrier system for Prep, Sealing, and Point-of-Sale. 30-minute rotation cycles.
Thermal & Biological Oversight
I. Critical Holding Temps: Proteins are maintained in Jacgood Chafing Units utilizing distilled water baths to ensure internal temps remain at 140°F+. Hourly infrared spot-checks are performed and logged.

II. Condensation Mitigation: Post-bake cooling for "From-Scratch" items occurs in a low-humidity stabilized environment. Moisture-seal labels are applied only after thermal stabilization to protect texture integrity.

III. Fluid Line Sanitation: Slushie and beverage dispensers undergo automated high-temp self-clean cycles pre-and-post event, utilizing food-grade antimicrobial agents.
Public Liability & Compliance Controls
Digital Allergen Transparency The Interactive QR Portal provides instant 1:1 access to the Master Ingredient Bible. Every pre-sealed item carries physical bold-text warnings for the "Big 9" allergens.
Site Redundancy 12/3 gauge power runs are secured via industrial cable ramps. Inflatable AV structures are rated for 25mph wind gusts with dual-tethering points.
