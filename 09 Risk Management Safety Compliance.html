PAGE 09 — RISK MANAGEMENT & SAFETY ARCHITECTURE

I. 4-Tier Cross-Contamination Barrier System

Gold Utensils: Dedicated to "Sweet Suite" (Churros/Brownies). Zero contact with savory profiles to ensure flavor integrity.
Silver Utensils: Dedicated to "Savory Staples" (Pretzels/Popcorn/Salts). Maintains the purity of dry-good inventory.
Red Utensils: Exclusive path for raw/high-heat proteins (Wings/All-Beef Dogs). Zero cross-over with non-meat surfaces.
White Protocol: Nitrile barrier system. Task-specific glove changes for Prep, Sealing, and POS; 30-min rotation cycles.

II. Thermal & Biological Compliance Logic

Active Protein Holding:
Jacgood Chafing Units utilize distilled water baths to maintain internal temperatures of 140°F+. Hourly infrared spot-checks are performed and logged via digital compliance ledger.

Condensation & Texture Control:
Post-bake cooling occurs in humidity-stabilized zones. Moisture-seal brand labels are applied only after thermal stabilization to prevent condensation and protect "From-Scratch" texture.

Sanitation Cycles:
Slushie and beverage dispensers undergo automated high-temp self-clean cycles pre-and-post event, utilizing food-grade antimicrobial agents to eliminate microbial buildup.

III. Site Redundancy & Public Liability

Digital Allergen Portal:
Interactive QR-based Master Ingredient Bible. Provides instant 1:1 transparency for the "Big 9" allergens.
Structural Safety:
Ballast-rated inflatable structures (25mph) secured with dual-tether 600D cabling. 12/3 gauge power runs protected by heavy-duty cable ramps.
