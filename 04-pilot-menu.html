Pilot Food & Beverage Overview

This page provides a comprehensive overview of the Pilot Day Food and Beverage offerings for the inaugural event. It outlines all items that will be served, the methodology for presentation and service, and highlights the operational planning behind each selection. While this page summarizes the menu, detailed pricing, revenue projections, and logistical information are presented in a dedicated Pilot Menu and Revenue Analysis page later in this packet. The menu has been curated to deliver a cinematic, drive-in movie theater experience while maintaining operational efficiency and maximum guest satisfaction.

Operational Scope

  • Wings: Properly portioned and served in eco-friendly food boats.
  • Okra and Corn: Individually plated to preserve freshness and optimal temperature.
  • Belgian Waffles: Served with toppings such as chocolate drizzle, whipped cream, and fresh fruit.
  • Popcorn and Cotton Candy: Served in custom branded packaging to reinforce the thematic experience.
  • Pretzels and Nachos: Pre-assembled and portion-controlled for efficient service.
  • Beverages: Slushies and other drinks provided in clear branded cups with lids.

Included Serving Elements

  • Branded food boats for wings, nachos, pretzels, and okra.
  • Condiment cups for sauces, dips, and toppings.
  • Eco-friendly napkins and utensils where required.
  • Slushy and beverage cups with lids and straws.
  • Small side trays for sauces and toppings at beverage stations.
