Pilot Menu Overview
Introduction
This page provides a comprehensive overview of the **Pilot Day Food & Beverage offerings** for the inaugural event. It outlines the full range of items that will be served, the presentation and service methodology, and highlights the careful planning behind every menu selection. While this page summarizes the menu, full **unit pricing, revenue projections, and logistical details** will be presented in a dedicated Pilot Menu & Revenue Analysis page later in this packet. The menu has been curated to deliver a cinematic, drive-in movie theater experience while maintaining operational efficiency and maximum guest satisfaction.
Operational Scope
The pilot menu is designed to be fully **modular and scalable**, ensuring a seamless food & beverage experience.
  • Wings: Properly portioned and served in eco-friendly food boats.
  • Okra & Corn: Individually plated, maintaining freshness and temperature.
  • Belgian Waffles: Served with toppings like chocolate drizzle, whipped cream, and fresh fruit.
  • Popcorn & Cotton Candy: Classic drive-in snacks in custom branded packaging.
  • Pretzels & Nachos: Pre-assembled and portion-controlled for fast service.
  • Beverages: Slushies and drinks served in clear branded cups with lids.
All pilot menu items are designed for efficiency in preparation, serving, and cleanup, while maximizing the guest experience.
Included Serving Elements
To ensure a professional and complete experience, the following **serving elements** will be included:
  • Custom branded food boats for wings, nachos, pretzels, and okra.
  • Condiment cups for sauces, dips, and toppings.
  • Eco-friendly napkins and utensils where required.
  • Slushy and drink cups with lids and straws.
  • Small side trays for sauces and toppings at beverage stations.
These items ensure every guest receives a **complete, enjoyable, and hygienic food experience** while maintaining brand consistency.
