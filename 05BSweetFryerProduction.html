1. SCRATCH FUNNEL CAKE FRIES (MIXER LOGIC)
Atomic Ingredients
  • 2c AP Flour
  • 2tbsp White Sugar
  • 1tsp Baking Powder
  • 1.5c Buttermilk
  • 2 Lg Eggs
  • 1tsp Vanilla
*REQUIRED: 10-count 2-pack Silicone Trays (3 sets)
Preparation: The Wet Froth
Step 1: Secure Whisk Attachment. Pour Buttermilk, Eggs, and Vanilla into the stainless steel Mixer Bowl.
Step 2: Engage Speed 6. Whip for 90 seconds until a thick foam "head" forms.
Step 3: Sift Flour, Sugar, and Baking Powder together in a separate bin while the mixer runs.
Mechanical: The Integration
Step 4: Reduce to Speed 1. Slowly rain the dry sift into the wet foam. Stop the motor once no white powder remains visible.
Step 5: Fill Silicone Trays immediately. Tap on counter. Freeze 4h until rock solid. Fry 350°F.
2. SCRATCH ATOMIC CHURROS (PADDLE ROUX)
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1c Water
  • 6tbsp Butter
  • 1tbsp Sugar
  • 1/4tsp Salt
  • 1c AP Flour
  • 3 Lg Eggs
Preparation: The Thermal Flash
Step 1: Boil Water, Butter, Sugar, and Salt in a pot. Once boiling, pour the entire scalding liquid directly into the Mixer Bowl.
Step 2: Secure Paddle Attachment. Add all Flour. Immediately engage Speed 2.
Step 3: The Paddle will "stir" the roux for 2m until a cohesive dough ball forms and the steam vents.
Mechanical: The Emulsion
Step 4: Increase to Speed 4. Stream in Eggs 1-by-1. Beat until the paste is glossy and elastic.
Step 5: Pipe 6" rods. Flash-freeze 1h. Thermal: Fry 375°F. Toss in 10:1 Cinnamon Sugar.
3. SCRATCH BAKING SODA PRETZELS (INDUSTRIAL KNEAD)
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1.5c Water (110°F)
  • 1tbsp Br Sugar
  • 1pkt Yeast
  • 4.5c Bread Flour
  • 2oz Melted Butter
  • Dried Rosemary
Preparation: The Bloom & Hook
Step 1: Load Warm Water and Brown Sugar into bowl. Sprinkle Yeast. Let sit 10m until active.
Step 2: Secure Dough Hook. Add Bread Flour, Butter, and Rosemary.
Step 3: Engage Speed 2. Knead for 8 full minutes. The machine must do all the work until dough is high-elasticity.
Mechanical: Stuffing & Finish
Step 4: Proof 60m. Roll into flat strips. Lay line of Cream Cheese or Colby Jack. Pinch shut and twist into knots.
Step 5: Submerge in Boiling Soda Bath for 30s. Bake 425°F. Finish with Butter/Salt.
