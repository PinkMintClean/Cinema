STATION ALPHA: THE SWEET FRYER (CHURRO & FUNNEL FOCUS)
1. SCRATCH-MADE CHURROS (PÂTE À CHOUX) Mechanical Prep: 1200W Planetary Mixer Required
Atomic Ingredients
  • Filtered Water
  • Unsalted High-Fat Butter
  • All-Purpose Flour (Sifted)
  • Farm-Fresh Large Eggs
  • Fine Sea Salt
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Cinnamon (Ceylon Grade)
  • Vanilla Bean Paste
Post-Cook Warming
Primary: Commercial Stainless Warming Tray (Dry Heat).
Secondary: Chafing Dish with indirect steam if volume exceeds 50 units/hour.
Industrial Prep Phases
X-2 DAY PREP: Boil water/butter. Incorporate flour via stovetop agitation. Transfer to mixer. Use Speed 2 for 2 mins to vent steam. Switch to Speed 4 and add eggs via splash-port until glossy emulsion is achieved. Pipe into 6" star-profile rods and flash-freeze.
Event-Day Field Staging
Setup Hour (T-60 mins): Pre-fry first 20 orders from frozen. Toss in Cinnamon-Sugar while oil-tack is active. Place in pre-heated warming tray. This ensures "Zero-Wait" service for the first wave of arriving vehicles. Drop fresh batches every 15 mins to maintain stock.
2. FUNNEL CAKE FRIES (CRYO-STAGED) Mechanical Prep: 1200W Whisk Aeration
Atomic Ingredients
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Double-Acting Baking Powder
  • Whole Milk (Cold)
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Large Eggs
  • Pure Vanilla Extract
  • Powdered Sugar (Dusting)
  • Pinch of Nutmeg
Post-Cook Bagging
Station Setup: Pre-fry 30 batches during vehicle ingress.
Bagging: Bag into S (8pc), M (12pc), and L (18pc) counts. Store bags upright in warming trays to maintain airflow and prevent sogginess.
Industrial Prep Phases
X-2 DAY PREP: Sift dry ingredients. Use 1200W Whisk at Speed 6 to combine dairy/eggs until frothy. Slow to Speed 1 and rain in dry mix via splash-port. Pipe into 4" "Fry" shapes on parchment. Freeze solid to lock the shape.
Event-Day Execution
Live Drop: Fry directly from frozen at 375°F. The frozen core ensures the fry stays buoyant and expands without absorbing excess oil. Dust with powdered sugar immediately upon exit using a fine-mesh shaker.
LENDER NOTE: By pre-bagging and staging in warming trays 60 minutes before the film starts, we solve the "Concession Rush" bottleneck. This mechanical prep allows a single operator to serve 100+ orders in the 20-minute peak window.
