SWEETERY LOGISTICS: EXHAUSTIVE PRODUCTION (CARDS 1-4)

1. SCRATCH-MADE CHURROS

1200W Engine: Paddle Attachment
Atomic Ingredients
  • Filtered Water
  • High-Fat Unsalted Butter
  • Sifted AP Flour
  • Fresh Grade A Eggs
  • Pure Vanilla Bean Paste
  • Fine Sea Salt
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Ceylon Cinnamon
  • Vegetable Fry Oil
Exhaustive Preparation & Phases
Phase 1: Boil water, butter, salt in heavy-bottom pot. Dump flour in at once; stir vigorously by hand until dough pulls from sides.
Phase 2: Transfer to mixer. Run Speed 2 for 3 mins to vent heat. Slowly add eggs at Speed 4 until a glossy "hook" forms. Fold in vanilla paste.
Phase 3 (Cryo): Pipe 6" star-profile rods on parchment. Flash-freeze for 4 hours. Once solid, transfer into labeled, airtight Ziploc bags for transport.
Service & Holding
Field Fry: 375°F Oil. Pre-fry 20 units T-60 mins. Toss in Cinnamon-Sugar while oil-tack is active.
Holding: Stainless Warming Tray (Dry Heat) at 155°F.
Sold: 3-count ($6) or 5-count ($9). Served in parchment gusset with dipping cup.

2. INDIVIDUALIZED FUNNEL CAKE FRIES

1200W Engine: Whisk Attachment
Atomic Ingredients
  • AP Enriched Flour
  • Whole Vitamin D Milk
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Farm Eggs
  • Baking Powder (Double-Act)
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Confectioners Sugar
  • Ground Nutmeg
  • Sea Salt
  • Refined Fry Oil
Exhaustive Preparation & Phases
Phase 1: Whisk milk, eggs, and vanilla in mixer at Speed 6 for 4 mins to create structural aeration.
Phase 2: In a separate bowl, sift dry goods twice to ensure Nutmeg and Salt distribution.
Phase 3: Reduce mixer to Speed 1. Rain in dry mix until "ribbon stage." Pipe 4" uniform rods onto trays.
Phase 4 (Cryo): Flash-freeze on trays. Transfer to Ziploc bags. Freezing locks the batter's moisture in for a fluffier "pop" when hitting hot oil.
Service & Holding
Field Fry: Fry directly from frozen at 375°F. Pre-fry S/M/L batch counts 45 mins prior to gates.
Holding: Warming Tray with vertical spacers to prevent stacking/sogging.
Sold: S (8) M (12) L (18). Served in paper food boats with heavy powdered sugar dust.

3. SCRATCH-MADE SOFT PRETZELS

1200W Engine: Dough Hook
Atomic Ingredients
  • High-Protein Bread Flour
  • Filtered Warm Water
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Light Brown Sugar
  • Clarified Butter (Melted)
  • Fine Sea Salt
  • Baking Soda (Bath)
  • Coarse Pretzel Salt
Exhaustive Preparation & Phases
Phase 1: Bloom yeast in warm water/sugar for 10 mins. Add to mixer with flour/butter. Knead at Speed 2 for 8 mins until elastic.
Phase 2: Bulk proof 60 mins. Portion into 5oz balls. Hand-roll 20" ropes and twist.
Phase 3: 30s dip in boiling water + Baking Soda (Alkaline Bath). This creates the "brown" pretzel skin. Flash-freeze solid.
Service & Holding
Field Bake: 425°F for 10-12 mins. Pre-bake 15 units 60 mins prior. Brush with melted butter immediately.
Holding: Humidity-Controlled Steam Warmer. Dry heat will ruin the crust.
Sold: Single Twist. Served in foil bag to retain moisture. Optional cheese dip.

4. PINEAPPLE UPSIDE-DOWN CAKE

1200W Engine: Paddle Attachment
Atomic Ingredients
  • Yellow Cake Base
  • Clarified Butter
  • Whole Vitamin D Milk
  • Reserved Pineapple Juice
  • Farm Large Eggs
  • Drained Pineapple Rings
  • Maraschino Cherries
  • Dark Brown Sugar
Exhaustive Preparation & Phases
Phase 1: Coat 9x13 pan base with melted butter. Pack 1/2" layer of dark brown sugar. Press pineapple rings and cherries into sugar.
Phase 2: In mixer, combine cake base, milk, juice, butter, and eggs at Speed 4 for 2 mins. Pour gently over fruit.
Phase 3: Bake. Must cool 100% in pan to allow sugar to "set" as a caramel glaze. Invert. Slice into 12 units. Wrap in branded cellophane.
Service & Holding
Staging: Individual units placed in bakery transport trays.
Holding: Ambient Acrylic Display Case. Visually showcases the glazed fruit to driving customers.
Sold: Single Slice ($5). Served on a gold-rimmed paper plate with a fork.
