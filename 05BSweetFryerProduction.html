1. SCRATCH ATOMIC CHURROS
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1c Filtered Water
  • 6tbsp Butter
  • 1tbsp Br Sugar
  • 1/4tsp Salt
  • 1c AP Flour
  • 3 Lg Eggs
PADDLE ATTACHMENT
Prep Process (Mixer)
Boil water/butter/sugar. Transfer to bowl. Speed 2 (Paddle): Add flour. Let mixer stir until steam clears and dough clears bowl sides.
Mechanical Process
Speed 4: Add eggs 1-by-1. Emulsify until glossy. Pipe 6" rods. Freeze 1h. Fry: 375°F. Finish: 10:1 Cinnamon Sugar.
2. SCRATCH FUNNEL CAKE FRIES
Atomic Ingredients
  • 2c AP Flour
  • 2tbsp White Sugar
  • 1tsp B.Powder
  • 1.5c Buttermilk
  • 2 Eggs / 1tsp Van
WHISK ATTACHMENT
Prep Process (Mixer)
Speed 6 (Whisk): Beat wet until frothy. Lower to Speed 1. Stream in dry ingredients through side-shield for hands-free blend.
Mechanical Process
Fill 10-count Silicone Trays. Tap to de-gas. Freeze 4h solid. Thermal: Fry 350°F. Finish with heavy 10X dusting.
3. SCRATCH BAKING SODA PRETZELS
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1.5c Warm Water
  • 1pkt Yeast / 1tbsp Sugar
  • 4.5c Bread Fl
  • 2oz Butter
  • Soda Bath
HOOK ATTACHMENT
Prep Process (Mixer)
Bloom yeast/sugar. Speed 2 (Hook): Add flour/butter. Let hook knead 8m until elastic. Proof 60m. Roll 20" ropes.
Mechanical Process
The Bath: Submerge 30s in boiling soda water. Thermal: Bake 425°F. Finish: Butter & Salt.
