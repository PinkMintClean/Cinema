Executive Overview

About Urban Cinematic Media

Urban Cinematic Media (UCM) curates immersive drive-in cinema experiences across Columbus, MS and surrounding communities, blending high-quality film screenings with modular culinary and entertainment assets designed for maximum scalability and revenue generation.

Our mission is to merge community-centric entertainment with operational excellence, ensuring unforgettable experiences while providing transparent, measurable returns for investors.

Operational Scope

The pilot event infrastructure is fully modular, reusable, and equipped with high-end audio-visual assets including:

  • 20-foot inflatable projector screen
  • Dual-range long-distance walkie talkies for operational coordination
  • Wireless plug-in microphone systems for hands-free communication
  • Concession stands with pre-packaged food assets and optimized inventory management
  • Full event flow documented from setup to teardown

This allows rapid scaling for future events, while maintaining high operational fidelity and minimal overhead.
