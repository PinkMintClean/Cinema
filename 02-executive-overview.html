Executive Overview

Urban Cinematic Media (UCM) is a modular, scalable outdoor cinema and culinary experience designed to operate profitably at low attendance thresholds while offering rapid expansion through menu density, sponsorships, and experiential upgrades.
Loan Ask
Requested Capital: $3,000
Purpose: Pilot Activation, Inventory, Licensing, Infrastructure
Liquidation Strategy
Loan recoverability demonstrated at 40% capacity through admissions, concessions, and on-screen advertising.
