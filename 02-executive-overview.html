Executive Overview

About Urban Cinematic Media

Urban Cinematic Media is a premium outdoor cinematic event experience designed to merge the vibrancy of community nightlife with the intimacy of a drive-in movie theater. Our mission is to provide an immersive entertainment event while maintaining high technical standards, operational efficiency, and scalable revenue streams. This portfolio demonstrates both our pilot implementation and our fully scaled vision, with detailed financials, operational blueprints, and logistical flow to support lender evaluation.

Operational Scope

The pilot event includes:

  • 20-foot inflatable projector screen
  • Long-range FN transmitter with dual wireless microphone packs
  • Portable concession stands serving chicken, corn, funnel cake fries, and specialty drinks
  • Parking logistics, solar lighting for staff, and waste management to protect the pasture
  • Secure ticketing and encrypted point-of-sale systems for admission and concessions

Every component is modular and reusable to support rapid scaling for future events, while ensuring regulatory compliance and operational efficiency.
