PAGE 10B — TRANSACTION ARCHITECTURE & FISCAL INTEGRITY

I. Staff-Directed Operational Layout

The "No-Touch" Policy:
For hardware longevity and hygiene safety, all primary devices (iPads/Tablets) are **Staff-Facing Only**. Customers have zero physical contact with the main operational interfaces.
Streamlined Food Entry:
A single centralized POS at the end of the culinary line. Staff perform rapid "Item Selection" (Small/Med/Large) based on the guest's tray, ensuring a 20-second finalization.

II. Secure Encryption & External Terminals

Urban Cinematic Media utilizes a **Divided-Interface POS System** (Square/iPad Integration) to maintain professional distance:

• External Readers: Card transactions are processed via mounted, Bluetooth-linked terminals. Guests utilize "Tap or Dip" functionality on the external reader, keeping the primary iPad secure behind a barrier.
• Integrated Audit Trail: On-site Bluetooth thermal printers generate dual receipts. One for guest verification; one for the "Nightly Master Folder" to ensure 100% fiscal accountability.
• Institutional Security: All data is bank-level encrypted. We strictly avoid unprofessional P2P apps (CashApp/Venmo) to maintain lender-grade financial standards.

III. Cash Handling & Denomination Control

Lockbox Integrity: Cash payments are instantly moved to an on-site steel-reinforced lockbox.

Fraud & Safety Mitigation: To protect staff and maintain site security, a **"No $100 Bill"** policy is enforced for all on-site transactions. This reduces the risk of counterfeit loss and ensures a high level of site-wide safety.

IV. Phase 2 Scaling: Automated Reporting

This digital infrastructure allows for **Real-Time Revenue Sync**. By the conclusion of the 3-hour window, we have a fully reconciled digital and physical audit trail, ready for immediate district compliance reporting.
