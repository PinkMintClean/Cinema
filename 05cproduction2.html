4. PINEAPPLE UPSIDE-DOWN CAKE

BATCH: 12 UNITS
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1 Box Yellow Hybrid
  • 1 Cup Whole Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Clarified Butter
  • 1 Cup Dark Brown Sugar
  • 20oz Drained Pineapple
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Pan Prep: Butter + sugar pack + fruit symmetry.
P2: Mixer Speed 4: Aerate 120s; pour over fruit.
P3: Bake to 205°F internal temperature.
Thermal & Service
Set: Ambient cool 100%.
Process: Invert; slice 12 units.
Service: Cellophane wrap.

5. DENSE SPECIAL DARK BROWNIES

BATCH: 9x13 PAN
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1 Box Fudge Hybrid
  • 1/4 Cup Dark Cocoa
  • 1tbsp Espresso Powder
  • 4oz Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Lg Eggs
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Mixer Speed 6: Cream butter/sugar/espresso.
P2: Speed 2: Blend in Cream Cheese until smooth.
P3: Fold dry base. Bake 325°F in steel.
Thermal & Logistics
Cure: 24hr Chill (Required).
Cut: Industrial 3x3 squares.
Service: Ambient display.

6. SCRATCH DENSE & CHEWY COOKIES

METHOD: TALL-CYLINDER
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1.5 Cups Bread Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Melted Butter
  • 1/4 Cup White Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Egg Yolks (No Whites)
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Mixer Low: Melted butter + sugars + yolks.
P2: Lowest Speed: Add dry; fold 1 cup chips.
P3: Scoop into "Tall Cylinders." Freeze 2h.
Thermal & Service
Thermal: Bake from frozen at 350°F.
Finish: Bakery-style density.
Vessel: Individual wax sleeve.
