4. PINEAPPLE UPSIDE-DOWN CAKE
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1 Box Yellow Hybrid
  • 1 Cup Whole Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Clarified Butter
  • 1 Cup Dark Br Sugar
  • 20oz Drained Pineapple
INVERSION METHOD
Prep Process (Mixer)
Pan Prep: Melt butter, spread. Pack brown sugar. Arrange pineapple rings symmetrically. In Mixer bowl, combine hybrid base and milk. Mixer Speed 4 (Paddle): Aerate 120s until pale and voluminous. Pour over fruit.
Mechanical Process
Bake: 350°F until internal temp 205°F. Cool 15m. Invert & Slice: Invert onto rack, then slice into 12 units. Service: Cellophane wrap each unit.
5. DENSE SPECIAL DARK BROWNIES
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1 Box Fudge Hybrid
  • 1/4 Cup Dark Cocoa
  • 1tbsp Espresso Powder
  • 4oz Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Lg Eggs
FUDGE CURE
Prep Process (Mixer)
Mixer Speed 6 (Paddle): Cream butter, sugars, and espresso until no crystals. Add cream cheese; beat Speed 2 until smooth. Sift cocoa into hybrid base.
Mechanical Process
Fold: Hand-fold dry into wet until just combined (dense texture). Bake: 325°F in steel pan. CRITICAL: Chill 24h. Cut into industrial 3x3 squares.
6. SCRATCH DENSE & CHEWY COOKIES
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1.5 Cups Bread Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Melted Butter
  • 1/4 Cup White Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Egg Yolks (No Whites)
TALL CYLINDER FORM
Prep Process (Mixer)
Mixer Low (Paddle): Cream melted butter, white sugar, brown sugar, and egg yolks until a "wet sand" texture. Lowest Speed: Gradually add bread flour.
Mechanical Process
Fold: Add 1 cup chips by hand. Do not overmix. Form: Scoop into "Tall Cylinders." Freeze 2h. Bake: From frozen at 350°F. Service: Individual wax sleeve.
