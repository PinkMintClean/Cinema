7. GOURMET SWEET POPCORN
Ingredients
  • 8c Popped Corn
  • 1/2c Butter
  • 1c Sugar
  • 3oz Jell-O Mix
Prep Process
Combine glaze in pan. Boil 2m. Mixer Speed 1: Toss corn until encapsulated.
Mechanical
Spread on tray. Drizzle chocolate. Bag in branded cellophane.
8. BELGIAN "CLOUD" WAFFLES
Ingredients
  • 2c AP Flour
  • 3/4c Milk
  • 1/2c Butter
  • 2 Eggs
Prep Process
Mixer Spd 8 (Whisk): Stiff peaks for whites. Fold into base batter gently.
Mechanical
Bake 400°F. Serve with 10X dusting. Direct to Upsell Station.
9. ATOMIC SUGAR CLOUDS
Floss Sugar
  • Caramel Floss
  • Black Cherry
  • Amazon Bases
Spin Logic
Pour sugar. Catch webs. Lift 12" for volumetric cloud pull. Cone only.
Clean Cycle
Scrape bowl every 5 units to prevent motor stall and sugar burning.
10. UPSELL DRIZZLE & TOPPING STATION
Thermal Glazes (110°F)
  • Vanilla Beam Drizzle
  • Alpine White
  • Salted Amber Caramel
Atomic Toppings
  • Strawberry Fusion
  • Chocolate Shreds
  • Crushed Peanut Dust
Service Protocol
Station Setup: All glazes must remain in thermal water bath to ensure fluid drizzle. Side-cup or direct application. Upsell: Suggest Strawberry + Vanilla combo for Waffles.
