7. GOURMET SWEET POPCORN (PRE-BAG)

POPCORN MACHINE / OVEN SET
Atomic Ingredients
  • 8 Cups Popped Corn
  • 1/2 Cup Butter / 1 Cup Sugar
  • 3oz Jell-O (Rasp/Blue)
  • Drizzle: Dark/White Melt
Detailed Process
Glaze: Boil sugar/butter/Jell-O 2m. Toss with corn.
Finish: While on cooling tray, drizzle with Chocolate/Vanilla melts.
Bag: ASAP once dry to the touch to lock in snap.
Flavors
Raspberry, Blueberry, Salted Caramel, Peanut. Bag in branded cellophane with gold ties.

8. BELGIAN "CLOUD" WAFFLES

WHISK-PEAKS / WAFFLE IRON
Atomic Ingredients
  • 2 Cups AP Flour
  • 3/4 Cup Whole Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Melted Butter
  • 2 Lg Eggs (Separated)
  • 1tbsp White Sugar
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Mixer Speed 8 (Whisk): Stiff peaks.
P2: Mix wet/dry; fold in yolks.
P3: Hand-fold whites into batter gently for airiness.
Thermal & Service
Cook: 400°F Iron. Serve hot.
Topping: Dust with powdered sugar.
Upsell: Direct to Station 10.

9. ATOMIC SUGAR CLOUDS

INDUSTRIAL SPIN MACHINE
Sugar Inputs
  • Amazon Floss Sugars
  • Caramel Floss Sugar
  • Black Cherry Sugar
The "Sugar Cloud" Tech
Spin: Catch webs on cone.
Pull: Lift 12" upward for volume.
Note: Keep dry. No glazes or liquids.
Cleaning
Scrape bowl every 5 units to prevent motor stall and sugar smoke.
