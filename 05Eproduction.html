11. HYBRID DENSITY CUPCAKES (INTERNAL MIX)
Atomic Inputs
  • Hybrid Vanilla Base
  • Smashed Oreo Debris
  • Buttermilk Liquid
  • Extra Egg Yolk
Prep Process (Mixer)
Mixer Speed 4: Blend base with supplements for high-density crumb. Divide batter. Internal Fusion: Fold Oreo debris into half the batch by hand. Ensure zero coring; the flavor is baked into the core.
Mechanical Process
Bake at 325°F. Cool completely. Finishing: Top with signature Cream Cheese frosting peaks (Vanilla, Oreo, or Salted Caramel). Serve in premium Atomic wrappers.
12. "THE MASHUP" COOKIE-BROWNIE BARS
Ingredients
  • Fudge Brownie Base
  • Chewy Cookie Dough
  • Vanilla Drizzle
  • Caramel Drizzle
Prep Process
Layer brownie base in steel pans. Press raw cookie dough chunks deep into the batter. Mechanical: Bake until the surface is a textured landscape of gold and dark fudge.
Service Protocol
No frosting required. Utilize the Upsell Station for hot Vanilla or Caramel drizzles. Slice into uniform industrial squares. Serve in wax-lined branded sleeves.
