11. HYBRID DENSITY CUPCAKES (MIXER INFUSED)
Atomic Inputs
  • Hybrid Vanilla Base
  • Smashed Oreo Debris
  • Buttermilk / Extra Yolk
Prep Process (Mechanical)
Step 1: Secure Paddle Attachment. Load Vanilla base, buttermilk, and yolks. Engage Speed 4 for 2m until dense.
Step 2: Reduce to Speed 1. Rain in the Oreo debris. Let the mixer fold for 30s. Zero Coring: The flavor is now physically integrated into the batter.
Mechanical Finish
Bake 325°F. Frosting Station: Load Cream Cheese into clean Mixer. Speed 8 (Whisk) until peaks are stiff. Divide and fold in Oreo sauce or Caramel. Pipe heavy.
12. "THE MASHUP" COOKIE-BROWNIE BARS
Ingredients
  • Fudge Brownie Base
  • Chewy Cookie Dough
  • Vanilla/Caramel Drizzles
Prep Process
Step 1: Mix Brownie base on Speed 2. Spread into pan. Step 2: Mix Cookie dough on Speed 1. Press chunks into the wet brownie landscape.
Service Protocol
Bake: 325°F. The cookie chunks must be gold while the brownie stays fudge-dense. Final Move: Heat Vanilla/Caramel drizzles at Upsell Station. Drizzle in a grid pattern.
