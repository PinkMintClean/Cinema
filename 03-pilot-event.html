Pilot Event Configuration Overview
This panel outlines the complete configuration required for our initial pilot event. Each component listed below is integral to delivering a seamless drive-in cinematic experience, from setup to operation. The pilot configuration focuses exclusively on the essentials, enabling us to test event logistics, operational flow, and attendee satisfaction while keeping scalability in mind.

20 ft Inflatable Projector Screen

  • Provides a cinematic viewing experience suitable for 50–75 attendees.
  • Modular and portable for rapid setup and breakdown within one hour.
  • Integrated anchor points for safety and stability in outdoor conditions.

FM Transmitter & Audio Distribution

  • Delivers synchronized audio to attendees' vehicles via radio frequency.
  • Ensures high-fidelity sound without disturbing surrounding neighborhoods.
  • Portable and scalable for future expansion in larger venues.

Lighting & Ambient Fixtures

  • String lights and portable LED uplighting to enhance ambiance without interfering with projection.
  • Adjustable dimming options for optimal visual and atmospheric balance.

Operational Logistics

  • Staging areas, ticketing, and parking workflow pre-planned for efficiency.
  • Staff deployment for concessions, security, and technical support included.
  • Clear signage and wayfinding for optimal attendee experience.
