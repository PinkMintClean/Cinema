7. BULK SOURCED SIDES (FRYER)
Atomic Inventory
  • Pre-Breaded Okra
  • Industrial Cut Fries
  • Atomic Savory Salt
Prep Logic
Zero Breading Rule: These items are pre-breaded/pre-cut. Portions must be bagged in the Commissary and kept at 0°F until the moment of service.
Machine: Deep Fryer (350°F)
Method: Drop from frozen. Fry Okra until it floats and reaches a deep gold. Fry Fries until stiff/crisp.
The Finish: Toss in steel bin with Atomic Savory Salt immediately.
8. ATOMIC SAVORY STUFFED PRETZELS
Atomic Inputs
  • Pretzel Dough (Pg 5B)
  • Pepper Jack Slices
  • Fresh Jalapeño Rounds
  • Garlic Butter Glaze
  • Rosemary Aromatics
The Mixer Phase
Step 1: Follow the Speed 2 (Dough Hook) process. Ensure Rosemary is integrated into the flour before the knead begins.
Step 2: After 1h proof, roll dough into long, flat industrial strips.
Mechanical Stuffing
Step 3: Lay Pepper Jack and Jalapeños down the center. Pinch the seam tight. Twist into the signature Atomic knot.
Step 4: 30s Soda Bath. Bake at 425°F. Finish with Garlic Butter.
9. EXTRA SOFT AROMATIC PRETZELS
Atomic Inputs
  • Pretzel Dough (High Hydration)
  • Fresh Rosemary
  • Melted Butter Bath
The "Extra Soft" Protocol
Method: Increase Water input by 10% during the mixer phase. Use Speed 2 for a full 10 minutes to develop maximum gluten elasticity.
The Aromatic Finish
Finish: Immediately upon exiting the oven, submerge the entire pretzel in a shallow Garlic-Rosemary Butter bath. Serve warm.
