PHASE 0: THE ATOMIC CLEANING PROTOCOL
Cleaning Inputs
  • White Vinegar
  • Coarse Salt
  • Ice Water
The Soft-Rub Method
Submerge all poultry. Execute a soft manual rub to remove impurities. Soak for 5 mins. Rinse 2x in fresh cold water until clear.
The Industrial Dry
Drain on perforated steel. Pat bone-dry using industrial towels. Moisture is the enemy of the Atomic Seasoning.
1. ATOMIC CHICKEN CHUNKS (GRILLED THIGH)
Inputs
  • Cleaned Thighs
  • Savory Brine
  • Liquid Smoke
Prep
The Cut: 1.5" Dice. The Cure: 24h Ziploc soak. Massage bag for maximum penetration.
Machine
Industrial Grill: High heat sear. Serve as "Chicken Fries" or premium Kid-Chunks.
2. ATOMIC SMOKED WINGS (GRILL)
Inputs
  • Cleaned Wings
  • Savory Marinade
  • Atomic Rub
Prep
12h Marinade in Ziploc. Keep chilled. Ensure full skin saturation.
Machine
Industrial Grill: Smoke until skin "snaps." Flash-char for visual heat.
3. ATOMIC BREADED WINGS (FRYER)
Inputs
  • Cleaned Wings
  • Seasoned Flour
  • HD Ziplocs
Shake & Freeze
Method: 10 wings/bag. Vigorously shake. Remove and flash-freeze on trays for "Atomic Crust" set.
Machine
Fryer (375°F): Drop from frozen. Fry until golden-armor crust forms.
