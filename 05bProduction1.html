URBAN CINEMATIC MEDIA: PRODUCTION BIBLE (PAGE 5B)

1. SCRATCH ATOMIC CHURROS

STATION 1
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1 Cup Filtered Water
  • 6tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1/4tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup AP Flour
  • 3 Lg Eggs
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Boil water, butter, sugar, salt. Add flour; stir until starch ball forms.
P2: Mixer Speed 4: Add eggs one at a time until glossy.
P3: Pipe 6" rods. Flash-freeze 1h.
Thermal & Service
Thermal: Fry 375°F (Frozen).
Finish: 10:1 Cinnamon Sugar.
Vessel: Branded Gusset Bag.

2. SCRATCH FUNNEL CAKE FRIES

STATION 2
Atomic Ingredients
  • 2 Cups AP Flour
  • 2tbsp White Sugar
  • 1tsp Baking Powder
  • 1.5 Cups Buttermilk
  • 2 Lg Eggs
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Mixer Speed 6: Beat eggs and buttermilk until frothy.
P2: Speed 1: Incorporate dry goods.
P3: Fill 10-count Silicone Trays. Freeze 4h.
Thermal & Service
Thermal: Fry 350°F until golden.
Finish: 10X Powdered Sugar.
Vessel: Paper Food Boat.

3. SCRATCH BAKING SODA PRETZELS

STATION 3
Atomic Ingredients
  • 1.5 Cups Warm Water
  • 1pkt Active Dry Yeast
  • 1tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 4.5 Cups Bread Flour
  • 2oz Butter (Melted)
  • 2/3 Cup Baking Soda (Bath)
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Bloom yeast 10m. Mixer Speed 2 (Hook): Knead 8m.
P2: Proof 60m. Roll 20" ropes; hand-twist.
P3: 30s Baking Soda boil. Flash-freeze.
Thermal & Service
Thermal: Bake 425°F (12-15m).
Finish: Butter & Coarse Salt.
Vessel: Foil Sleeve.
