1. SCRATCH ATOMIC CHURROS

1200W: PADDLE-VENT
Ingredients
  • Filtered Water
  • 6tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 3 Lg Eggs
  • Vanilla Paste
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Boil wet; stir in flour vigorously until starch ball forms.
P2: Mixer Speed 4: Add eggs 1-by-1 until glossy.
P3: Pipe into 6" star rods. Flash-freeze 1h; Ziploc seal.
Thermal & Service
Fry: 375°F from frozen state (2-4m).
Finish: 10:1 Cinnamon Sugar.
Hold: 155°F Dry Heat.

2. SCRATCH FUNNEL CAKE FRIES

WHISK-AERATION
Ingredients
  • AP Flour
  • 2tbsp White Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Buttermilk (Sub)
  • 2 Lg Eggs
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Mixer Speed 6: Beat wet until frothy.
P2: Sift dry; mix Speed 1 until pourable.
P3: Fill 10-count Silicone Molds. Freeze 4h until rock solid.
Thermal & Service
Fry: 350°F until golden.
Finish: Heavy 10X Dusting.
Vessel: Paper Food Boat.

3. SCRATCH BAKING SODA PRETZELS

DOUGH HOOK
Ingredients
  • High-Protein Flour
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Brown Sugar
  • Baking Soda (Bath)
  • Coarse Sea Salt
Prep & Mechanical
P1: Bloom 10m. Speed 2: Knead 8m (Hook).
P2: Proof 60m. Roll 20" ropes; twist.
P3: 30s Baking Soda boil to set crust. Flash-freeze on trays.
Thermal & Service
Heat: Bake 425°F or Flash-Fry.
Finish: Clarified butter/salt.
Vessel: Foil Sleeve Bag.
