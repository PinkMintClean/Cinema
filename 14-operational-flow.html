Event-Day Operational Sequence

  1. Site setup: screen, projector, transmitter, lighting
  2. Concessions prep & hot hold verification
  3. Gate opens: ticket scanning & payment processing
  4. Parking guidance via walkie coordination
  5. Pre-show announcements via FM mixer
  6. Movie playback with live monitoring
  7. Concession runners activated for VIP zones
  8. Exit sequence announced clearly via FM
  9. Controlled vehicle exit from pasture

This sequence is designed to minimize congestion, maximize concession flow, and maintain safety during all phases of the event.
