Risk Controls & Safeguards

Operational Risk

  • Redundant equipment planning and modular menu design
  • Pre-event prep cycles reduce on-site dependency
  • Staff cross-training minimizes labor disruption

Financial Risk

  • Weekly cash sweep allocations for debt reserve
  • Inventory purchased in scalable tranches
  • High-margin items prioritized during peak hours

Environmental & Attendance Risk

  • Weather contingencies and rescheduling protocol
  • Indoor-adaptable components in later phases
  • Multi-city footprint reduces geographic dependence
