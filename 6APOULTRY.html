PRODUCTION BIBLE // PAGE 6A (POULTRY)

PHASE 0: THE ATOMIC CLEANING PROTOCOL
ALL CHICKEN (Wings & Thigh Chunks) must undergo the following before prep:
Cleaning Solution
  • Distilled White Vinegar
  • Coarse Sea Salt
  • Ice Cold Water
The Process
The Scrub: Submerge poultry. Give a gentle "soft rub" to every piece. Let sit 5 mins. The Rinse: Drain and rinse 2x with fresh cold water.
The Drying
The Drain: Transfer to perforated trays. Pat completely dry with industrial paper towels. Proceed to Step 1.
1. ATOMIC CHICKEN CHUNKS (GRILLED THIGH)
Inputs
  • Cleaned Thigh Chunks
  • Savory Brine
  • Poultry Seasoning
Commissary Prep
Step 1: The Soak. Massage chunks in Ziploc with Savory Brine/Smoke. 24-Hour Cure Required.
Machine
Industrial Grill: Toss in dry rub. Sear until smoky and charred.
2. ATOMIC SMOKED WINGS (GRILL)
Inputs
  • Cleaned Jumbo Wings
  • Savory Marinade
  • Dry Rub
Commissary Prep
The Soak: 12h Marinade in Ziploc. Ensure full skin contact for flavor absorption.
Machine
Industrial Grill: Smoke until skin snaps. Flash-char for visual heat.
3. ATOMIC BREADED WINGS (FRYER)
Inputs
  • Cleaned Jumbo Wings
  • Seasoned Flour
  • Heavy Duty Ziplocs
The Shake & Freeze
Step 1: 10 wings/bag + Flour. Vigorously shake. Remove, shake off excess, and flash-freeze on trays.
Machine
Deep Fryer (375°F): Drop from frozen. Fry until crispy gold.
